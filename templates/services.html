Government Services

Click on each service to see the detailed procedure, requirements, and apply for the service:

  • Agriculture Subsidies

    • Procedure:

    Farmers must submit an application along with land records to avail subsidies. Application forms can be downloaded from the official agricultural department website or collected from the local office.

    Requirements:

    Required documents include land ownership papers, Aadhar card, and proof of crop cultivation.

    Apply Now
  • Crop Insurance

    • Procedure:

    Farmers need to enroll in the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana through their local agricultural office or online portal.

    Requirements:

    Land ownership proof, bank account details, and crop details must be submitted.

    Apply Now
  • Loan Assistance

    • Procedure:

    Farmers can apply for loans from registered banks under government-approved agricultural loan schemes. Application can be submitted online or at the nearest bank.

    Requirements:

    Documents required include land records, bank account details, and credit history.

    Apply Now
  • Fertilizer Subsidy

    • Procedure:

    Farmers are eligible to receive subsidized fertilizers from authorized centers by submitting valid identification and farming documents.

    Requirements:

    Aadhar card, land ownership papers, and crop details are required for eligibility.

    Apply Now
